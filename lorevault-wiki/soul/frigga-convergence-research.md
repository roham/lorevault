# Frigga-Soul Research: Convergence Strategy (Cycles 25-30)

## Hybrid Viability

### C→B (Battle feeds Set Completion) — RECOMMENDED
- Battle as acquisition channel is exactly what Pokemon TCG Pocket does (packs AND wonder pick)
- Set completion (binder) is a passive scoreboard, not a second game — same emotional register as battle wins
- The binder slot filling is a micro-reward that compounds with battle wins
- Conditions: battle rewards must be set-specific, binder visible from post-battle, finishing mode triggers regardless of acquisition source

### C→A (Battle feeds Story Unlocks) — RISKY
- Works only if battle is the gate, not the destination ("I fight to unlock chapters")
- Risk: two emotional peaks in one session (battle victory + story reveal) fractures Aha-moment
- Story unlocking requires active engagement (reading), creating cognitive load on top of battle
- Higher production overhead (writing, art, localization)

### Hybrid Single-Verb Test
"I battle to collect cards and complete sets." Two clauses but one verb — subordinate clauses are consequences of battling. This passes.

## Convergence Patterns from Real Apps

### Pokemon TCG Pocket
- Did NOT hybridize at launch — launched one verb ("feel the card"), proved retention, THEN surfaced battle
- Sequence matters more than combination

### Marvel Snap
- Cut explanation, not mechanics. Complexity hidden behind progressive disclosure
- "Snap" (doubling stakes) is the emotional identity — everything else is infrastructure

### Duolingo
- Serialized complexity: new users see only lessons + streak. Stories at level 5. Leagues opt-in.
- The verb is about identity ("I'm a person who does Duolingo every day"), not product category

### Wordle
- Pure single-verb caps at acquisition. Zero retention/monetization depth. Anti-pattern for collectibles.

## The "Second Verb" Rules

**Succeeds when:**
- Instrumental (verb 2 produces verb 1 rewards — Clash Royale)
- Sequential (verb 2 happens before/after, not during — Stardew Valley)
- Same emotional register (both produce the same feeling)

**Fails when:**
- Equal hierarchy (Artifact: buy + draft + battle = three verbs = dead in 6 months)
- Mismatch between stated and actual verb (Diablo Immortal)
- Third verb invented to paper over weakness (NBA Top Shot's "showcase")

## Fastest Path to 50+

**Option B (Hybridize) recommended: ceiling 52-55/70**

Core loop: Battle to earn cards (C) → cards fill binder (B) → completing set unlocks world event (A as meta-layer)
- One verb: "Battle"
- One scoreboard: the binder
- One long-arc reward: world unlock
- One monetization surface: "buy the missing card to complete the set"

## Cycles 25-30 Plan

- **25-26**: Adopt C→B hybrid. Battle earns cards, cards fill sets. Vision A's world map as meta-layer.
- **27-28**: Aha-Moment surgery. Card reveal must include: rarity, set membership, gap-fill status. All in under 3s.
- **29-30**: Final scoring with weighted dimensions (Progressive Disclosure + Aha-Moment at 1.5x).
- **Cut**: Story-chapter system deferred to Season 2. Highest production overhead, most deferrable payoff.
