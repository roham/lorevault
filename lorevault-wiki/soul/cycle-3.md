- **8/10 on First-Minute Emotion = unsolicited narration**. If the user doesn't tell someone about their experience, it's not an 8. This becomes our north star for emotional resonance.
- **Apple editorial penalizes feature completeness** — overbuilt apps rarely win App of the Day. They select for narrative arc in first use and "cultural specificity" (feels made for someone, not everyone).
- **Pokemon TCG Pocket measures pack satisfaction via post-reveal dwell time** > 4 seconds = positive affect. Concrete, measurable.
- **Core scoring question**: "Strip the branding — does the experience still feel intentional?"

## The One Question
