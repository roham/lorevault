## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

A letter from Dr. Seward, written on the twentieth.

Lucy Westenra died in the night of September 20. The letter was brief: she had passed peacefully; Van Helsing was with her; everything that could be done had been done.

He read the letter. He gave it to Mina.

He noted in his journal: *21 September — letter from Seward. Lucy Westenra died on the night of 20 September 1897.*

He looked at this notation. He had written the full date — the 20th of September, 1897. He had not written it with any particular intention; he had written it the way one writes a date of death, because a date of death should be written completely.

He did not add anything to the notation. He sat with the journal for a while. He thought about Lucy Westenra, whom he had never met. He knew her through Mina's letters. She had been a good correspondent; she had read landscapes; she had liked to sit in the clifftop churchyard at Whitby. He had tracked her condition across seven entries since August 29. The arc was now closed.

He wrote: *I have noted this.*
