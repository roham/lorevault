## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

A brief note in the morning post.

Mina writes quickly, not her usual letter: Lucy had a very bad night. Van Helsing has been sent for again from Amsterdam. Dr. Seward is with her. Mina says she is well herself and does not want him to worry; she will write more when she knows more.

He read the note twice.

He noted in his journal: *10 September — note from Mina. Lucy worse last night. Van Helsing summoned back from Amsterdam.* He looked at the previous entry. *9 September: better (provisional).* He wrote: *The provisional is now the operative notation.*

He thought about this. He had written "provisional" on September 9 because he could not claim certainty; accuracy required marking the uncertainty. The uncertainty had been correct. The word had done its work.

He did not feel any particular satisfaction in this. He wrote the note and set it with the letters.

He went to the office and worked through the morning. There was nothing he could do about Whitby from Exeter. He knew this. He worked through the afternoon as well.
