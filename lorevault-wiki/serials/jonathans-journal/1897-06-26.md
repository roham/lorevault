## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

Through the unlocked door he found a long corridor and, at its end, a chapel.

The chapel was in disuse — the pews had been removed and the floor was covered with wooden crates of the kind he had seen loaded and dispatched from the courtyard. He counted them. Fifty. He moved along the rows, recording their arrangement: five ranks, ten deep, roughly uniform spacing. He noted that several were open or had lids not fully secured. The smell was the same he had noted when watching the courtyard on June 22.

He looked into one of the open crates. It contained soil. Dark, dense, cohesive — not the soil he would have associated with Transylvania, though he could not have stated with precision what Transylvanian soil looked like. He noted the quality. He wrote it down.

He continued counting. He arrived again at fifty. He double-counted and arrived at fifty-two. He settled on fifty as his figure, allowing that some crates may have been partially obscured.

He wrote: *26 June — the chapel. Approximately 50 crates. Soil. The smell from the June 22 courtyard loading. I have now seen both ends of the supply.*

He had seen the loading on June 22 without understanding what was being loaded. He now had the crates and their contents. He still did not have the purpose.
