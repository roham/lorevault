## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

More crates left the castle.

He watched from the east window in the late morning. The courtyard was active — workers moving with the same quick, unceremonious purpose he had observed on June 22. He counted: eleven crates on this movement, added to the seventeen he had recorded on June 22. Twenty-eight total in the outbound inventory.

He had counted fifty in the chapel on June 26. Twenty-eight departed. Twenty-two remaining, if his counts were accurate — and he had double-checked both.

He wrote: *2 July — eleven more crates. Total departed: 28. Chapel stock at time of count: approx. 50. Remainder: approx. 22.*

He noted: the Count was conducting a removal. The property was being transferred — not the title, which Jonathan had already documented for the Count's London acquisition, but the physical contents of the Castle. The Count was moving.

He was the only thing in the Castle that was not moving.

He added this to the ledger without comment. He was past the stage of commenting on the things he added to the ledger.
