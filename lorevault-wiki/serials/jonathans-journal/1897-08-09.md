## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He gave Mina the journal.

He had decided this on August 3. He had not wavered from the decision; he had kept the journal in the coat and the coat in the wardrobe and had not re-read it, as decided. He took it out of the coat on August 9 and placed it on the table between them.

He said: I kept a record. Everything that happened is in this. I cannot read it yet. You may read it if you choose.

She picked it up. She turned it over. She did not open it immediately.

She said: when you are ready, we will read it together.

He wrote — in the journal, after she had put it back on the table: *9 August — gave Mina the journal. She said: when you are ready, we will read it together. I wrote this entry after. The journal was out of my hands and then it was back in my hands.*

He noted: *when you are ready* was a terminus specified — a departure date that was his to set, not someone else's. He had been receiving unspecified termini since June 19. This one was his.
