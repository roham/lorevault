## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

At ten in the morning, Jonathan tried the door at the end of the south corridor.

He was methodical about it. He had drawn a rough map of the floors he could reach — the library, the dining room, the corridor with the portrait, two guest rooms he had not been invited to enter. The south corridor terminated at a door he had passed six times without trying. He told himself he had simply never needed to go that direction.

He stood in front of it for a moment. Then he tried it.

It was locked.

He stood for another moment. He noted this in the journal in a single line: *South corridor door — locked.* He drew a small bracket on the map and wrote inside it: *locked.* He went back to the library. He spent the rest of the morning reading a history of the Carpathians from 1684.

He did not try the east corridor door that afternoon. He had decided to be systematic. One door per day.

He did not mention, in the journal, that there were eleven more.
