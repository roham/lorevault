## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He waited.

He had decided July 20: *tomorrow night or the night after, depending on conditions*. On July 21, in the late afternoon, he assessed the conditions. No movement outside. The courtyard was empty. The sky to the east was clear. The third course appeared dry from the window. No sound in the corridor.

The conditions were satisfactory.

He ate. He drank water from the ewer. He put on the coat. He put the journal inside the coat, against his chest. He sat in the chair.

He wrote: *21 July — conditions satisfactory. I am ready. I am going tonight.*

He added, below this: *If this is the last entry — the journal is the record. The coat contains the letters. The brief is in the cover. The record is complete.*

He reviewed this. He had been writing this journal as though someone else might read it. On July 21 he was, for the first time, explicitly writing for that possibility. He had been doing it since May 3 without knowing that was what he was doing.

He closed the journal. He put it inside the coat.

He waited for dark.
