## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

That morning Jonathan shaved using a small traveling mirror he had brought in his bag. He had not used it since arriving — the Castle had no mirrors anywhere. He had not thought about this until he was looking at his own face and found it strange in a way he could not account for. He did not look worse than he expected. He looked exactly as he expected. He could not say why this troubled him.

He put the mirror face-down in the bag after he finished. He told himself it was to protect the glass.

The Count came to breakfast that morning for the first time in three days. He sat across from Jonathan and watched him eat without eating himself. Jonathan poured two cups of tea. The Count did not touch his. Jonathan did not remark on this. He had been noticing things he did not remark on. He was developing a list.
