## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He wrote a letter to Mina.

Not inside the journal (July 7's address in the second person). Not on Castle writing paper in his professional hand (June 21's advance copies). On ordinary letter paper, in the social hand, in the first person, from Bistritz. He wrote: *Dear Mina, I am at the Golden Krone Hotel in Bistritz. I am well — better than I have been. I have been ill. I am better now. I will explain everything when I see you. Please come if you can. I need to see you.*

He read it back. He had said *I will explain everything when I see you* and did not know whether this was true. He did not know whether he could explain everything. He did not know whether everything was explainable.

He kept the sentence. He gave the letter to Sister Agatha for posting.

He wrote in the journal: *1 August — wrote to Mina. Posted. I said I would explain everything. I do not know whether I can.*

## Tether Note

*The first letter sent* — Jonathan writes his first outgoing communication since the Castle: not in the journal, not in the professional hand, not in advance of events — but a direct letter in the social voice, posted. He says "I will explain everything" and immediately qualifies it in the journal. The gap between what is said in correspondence and what is true in the record: a pattern the journal has tracked since June 21 (the advance letters). This letter is the inversion — true in register, uncertain in content.
