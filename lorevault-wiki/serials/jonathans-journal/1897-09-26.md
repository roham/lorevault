## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

They walked in the afternoon.

He and Mina walked from the house down to the cathedral close and back — about an hour, at a comfortable pace. This is a walk they have done before; he knows the route well. It was a good day for walking: mild, some cloud, the light ordinary and adequate.

He had not walked with Mina since before August 27. They had walked in Budapest, before they left; they had walked in various cities on the return journey. Since returning to Exeter they had been mostly inside — the house, the office, the brief trips to Mrs. Hawkins and back. Today they walked.

He noted in his journal: *26 September — walked to the cathedral close and back with Mina. Approximately one hour.*

He thought about whether to add anything to this notation. He looked at it. He thought: one hour at the cathedral close with Mina, in September, after everything. He did not add anything. The notation was accurate within its scope and the scope was right. He found the walk was a thing the journal could contain completely in two lines, and that two lines were enough.
