## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

They came at night.

He heard them in the corridor — not footsteps, not the ordinary sounds of movement, but a presence that manifested first as a change in the quality of silence outside his door and then as something he could hear without knowing the category of sound it was. He had been awake. He remained very still.

His door held. He had not locked it — he had no key. The door was simply a door that held.

He stayed in the chair until it was light.

He wrote: *17 July — they came to the corridor. The door held. I did not leave the chair until morning.*

He did not write more than this. He reviewed the entry afterward and found it accurate. He noted: he had previously written about the encounter in the locked room (June 17) with considerable precision — the dress, the manner, the absence of shadow, the dual emotion, the Count's phrase. He had no precision available for this entry. The entry was what it was.

He counted: the second time. The first had been June 17. The second was July 17. Exactly one month.
