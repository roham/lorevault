## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He had been in the north library without knowing he had walked there.

He became aware of his location when the light changed — the afternoon light in the north library had a particular angle that he had noted when he first found the room, and that angle was what brought him back to the awareness of where he was. He was seated in the chair in the north library. He had been in his own room when he last had a clear sense of location.

He did not know how long ago that had been. He did not know the route he had taken; he did not know whether he had walked directly or wandered.

He had the journal with him. He had not, as far as he could recall, decided to bring it.

He wrote the entry there, in the north library, which he had not done before: he had always written at his own desk. He wrote: *14 June — north library. Arrived: unclear. Journal present.*

He noted that this was the same room in which he had found the R.M.S. note. He noted this without knowing why it seemed relevant.
