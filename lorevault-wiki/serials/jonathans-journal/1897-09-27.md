## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He went to the office after church.

There was nothing urgent waiting. He had cleared the urgent correspondence on Friday; Saturday he had walked; today there was no professional reason to go to the office. He went anyway.

He worked on a matter that did not require his attention until Wednesday. He organized the files. He read through a precedent he had been meaning to revisit. He made notes. He was there for two hours.

He noted in his journal: *27 September — went to the office Sunday afternoon. No urgent reason. Two hours.*

He thought about why he had gone. He found, on consideration, that the office is a place he understands thoroughly — the papers are where they are supposed to be; the work proceeds by rules he knows; the outcome of his efforts is legible and checkable. He had gone on Sunday because it was a place where the shape of things was clear.

He noted: *The office is a place where the shape of things is clear.* He found this was true and not something he had previously written down. He thought he might continue going to the office on Sundays when things were less clear elsewhere. He did not note this plan because plans belong in plans, not journals.
