## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He went outside.

Not the Castle courtyard, observed through a window. Not the mountain road walked in a fever-adjacent state, moving because stopping meant not moving. The street in front of the Golden Krone Hotel, Bistritz, mid-morning, sun at the correct height for mid-morning in early August.

He stood outside the hotel door for some time. He noted: the street had sound. People moving; a cart; somewhere, a dog; the specific acoustic quality of an inhabited street at an ordinary hour. He had not been in an inhabited street at an ordinary hour since Munich, May 1.

He walked to the corner and back. He went inside.

He wrote: *6 August — walked to the corner and back. The street has the sound of an inhabited street. I was outside voluntarily.*

He noted: *voluntarily*. He had left the Castle on July 22 by necessity. He had walked the mountain road by necessity. This was different. He had chosen to be outside because being outside was possible and he wanted to know what it felt like.

It felt like a corner and back.
