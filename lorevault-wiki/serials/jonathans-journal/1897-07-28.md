## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He reached Bistritz.

He did not know, precisely, how he had managed the final distance; the fever had been running through July 27 and had not fully cleared. He had walked and had been helped at intervals by people on the road who appeared to understand that he needed direction. He had said *Bistritz* and been pointed in the correct direction three times.

The Golden Krone hotel. He recognised it. He had stayed here on May 4, his last night before the Castle — the night the proprietress had looked at him with an expression he had documented without interpreting. He had interpreted it since. He was back.

He wrote: *28 July — Bistritz. The Golden Krone. The same hotel. I am in a room. I am very tired. The same building.*

He noted: the same building. He had left Bistritz on May 4 as a professional on an engagement. He had returned on July 28 as a man who had walked down from the Borgo Pass with a coat full of letters and a journal under his arm. The building was unchanged. He was not.
