## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

A letter from Mina.

Professor Van Helsing arrived yesterday from Amsterdam. Mina says he is a very impressive man — she does not elaborate on the nature of this impression, but the sentence is emphatic; she wanted him to know this. Van Helsing examined Lucy. This morning Lucy has somewhat more color than she has had in a week. Mina says she does not know what Van Helsing did or prescribed, but Lucy looks better today and that is what matters.

He read this and was relieved.

He wrote in his journal: *9 September — Van Helsing arrived from Amsterdam. Lucy has more color today. Mina says he is impressive.* He paused and looked back through the entries. Postcard August 29: well. Letter September 2: unwell. Letter September 3: worse. Now September 9: better. He counted: four entries about Lucy since August 29. He noted: *Four entries. Arc: well → unwell → worse → better (provisional).*

He added the word *provisional* because he did not know if the improvement would hold. He found this was accurate — he did not know, and accuracy required marking the uncertainty.

He set the letter with the others on the shelf. He would note the next entry when it came.
