## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

A letter from Mina.

Lucy is very pale again. Dr. Seward and Professor Van Helsing are both attending. Mina writes: *they seem worried.* She says she thinks she should stay through at least the end of the week. She does not say what they are worried about. She does not say what Van Helsing has determined. She writes: *they are doing everything that can be done.* She says Lucy has been more peaceful today.

He read the letter carefully.

He noted in his journal: *13 September — letter from Mina. Lucy very pale, both Seward and Van Helsing attending. Mina's phrasing: "seem worried," "everything that can be done," "more peaceful today." None of these phrases give a clear status.* He looked at his own notation. He wrote: *The letter does not know what is wrong. Mina does not know what is wrong. The doctors may know what is wrong, but Mina does not have this information, and therefore I do not.*

He replied to Mina. He said: stay as long as Lucy needs you. He said he was well. He said she should write when she had more to report, and not to worry about writing when she did not.

He noted the reply in his journal: *Told her to stay.* He did not note the phrasing further.
