## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

They were married in Budapest.

He had not, previously, written an entry on the day of the event. He had consistently written at a slight distance from events — the entry for an event was usually written after the event, sometimes hours after, sometimes the following day. He wrote this entry on August 11, the same day, in the evening, while Mina was writing in her own diary at the same table.

He wrote: *11 August — married today. Budapest. Mina and I at the same table; she is writing in her diary; I am writing in mine. The same table.*

He noted: they were writing at the same table. This was a new configuration. He had written this journal in the Castle's rooms (alone), on the mountain road (alone), in the village and at the Golden Krone (alone, or with Sister Agatha present but not writing). He was now writing alongside someone also writing.

He thought: the journal would be different from here. He did not know exactly how. He wrote this down.
