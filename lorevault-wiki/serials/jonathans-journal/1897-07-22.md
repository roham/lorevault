## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He went.

He did not write this entry at the Castle. He wrote it afterward — he could not say where; he had been moving through the forest approach and then the pass road for a period he could not measure precisely (his watch was in the coat pocket; he did not check it; he was moving). He wrote it when he was far enough from the Castle that he could stop and was reasonably certain the stopping was safe.

The descent: the stone courses held. Third course from the bottom: dry, as he had assessed. He had reached the courtyard, crossed it, found the castle gate ajar — not open, but not fully latched; a gap sufficient to pass through. He had not asked why the gate was ajar. He had passed through it.

The forest. The road. Moving.

He wrote: *22 July — I have left the Castle. I am writing this from the mountain road. The gate was ajar. I do not know why. I have the coat and the journal.*

He noted: he did not know why the gate was ajar. He had three hypotheses; he weighted them equally; he would not resolve them in this entry. He had been noting things he could not resolve since May 3. The ledger would continue.

He was outside. He was writing. The date was July 22.
