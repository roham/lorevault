## Lampblack Detail — daemon annotation (not in Stoker)

He filed two completed matters and had a quiet afternoon.

Before closing the journal for the day, he turned back through the recent entries. The postcard: *Arrived safely. Lucy met her. Lucy looked well.* The first letter: *Sleeping badly, pale. Dr. Seward attending.* The second letter: *Pallor worsened. Sleepwalking. Van Helsing may come.* He had not intended to create a record of Lucy Westenra's condition. He had been noting what Mina told him, entry by entry, and the entries had accumulated into something that functioned like a case record.

He recognized this. He has been tracking Lucy's condition as he tracked things in the Castle.

He wrote in his journal: *5 September — reviewed the Lucy entries. Three entries since August 29: well, then unwell, then worse. I have been tracking this. I notice I am tracking this.*

He sat with this for a moment. He thought: I do not know what I am tracking toward. In the Castle he had known what he was tracking toward — a terminus, a departure date, a sufficient record for Mina if he did not get out. He does not know what he is building here.

He closed the journal and left the office.
