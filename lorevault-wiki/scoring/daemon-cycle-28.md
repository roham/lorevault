## What shipped

1. **Monthly Collector Pass** — 30-tier reward track with XP thresholds (150 XP per tier). Free rewards rotate: XP, cards, packs. Premium tier rewards: parallels, badges, legendary at T30. Monthly auto-reset. Preview on home page, full view on profile.
2. **Weekly Challenges** — 3 challenges per week from 8-challenge pool, seeded by week number. Targets: win battles, collect cards, forge, open packs, earn XP. Progress bars on home page.
3. **Collector Milestones** — 10 permanent achievements: Century (100 cards), Vault Keeper (200), XP Sovereign (L20), Forge Master (10 forges), Pyromaniac (10 burns), Dedicated (14-day streak), Legendary Hoarder (10 legendaries), Set Collector (3 sets), Full Spectrum (all scarcities), Marathon (30-day streak). Each grants a selectable title prefix.
4. **Season 2 Tease** — "The Underworld — Coming Soon" banner with silhouetted character placeholders on home page. Creates FOMO and anticipation.
