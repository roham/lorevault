## 1. Universe identity — Pane-of-Fate · White/Red register · Cosmic-scale debt

**This Pane is Fate.** Not destiny — the word is banned (see §15). *Moira* — the share-out, the portion-cut. Every figure here operates under a portion already measured, a thread already spun, a bowl already filled. The cards know what the figures don't. The Pythia knows what the cards don't, and she will not say so plainly.

**Register split: White and Red.** Greek Myth runs on a binary the other four S1 Universes do not have. **White** = the marble-and-libation register (temple, oracle, chiton, olive-branch, chorus in third-person plural). **Red** = the pottery-and-blood register (Achaean spear, sacrificial bowl, pomegranate-juice, the wound that does not close). White is the Pane in repose; Red is the Pane in the act. Every Set must pull from both. All-White is a frieze; all-Red is a butcher's stall.

**Cosmic-scale debt.** Greek Myth is the only S1 Pane where the *gods themselves* carry the debt. Sherlock pays — London does not. The Big Bad Wolf pays — the Forest does not. Here, Zeus owes Demeter a daughter; Hades owes Persephone six months; Prometheus owes the eagle a liver every dawn; Sisyphus owes the stone the slope, in perpetuity. **Every Greek Myth Moment must read as the inside of a ledger that began before the figure was born and continues after.** This is the cosmic-scale no other S1 Pane runs at — what buys us AETHER-shell native rights (§7).

**Pane-of-Fate, the property's own statement.** This is the Pane where every figure carries a portion already cut, a thread already spun, a bowl already filled. Bernini-marble surface; pomegranate-red and obsidian-black register; oil-paint depth, never webtoon-flat. The cosmic ledger is the picture-plane. The chorus speaks; the named figure participates; the Pythia answers slowly and says less than the supplicant came for. Binding.

(Competitive-positioning notes — what this Pane is *not* — are catalogued in §15 Voice Register Rules and the negative-prompt block in §13 / §A.4. Per V2 Review Rewrite 4: §1 lands as the property's own statement, not as a defense against another property. The reader meets the Pane on its own terms; the un-Lore-Olympus rule moves to §15 production-binding voice register, where commissioning leads need it.)

## 5. Typography

**Display register — classical-stele inscription.** Card titles render in stoichedon-grid all-capitals — 5th-century Athenian inscriptional register. Letterforms pre-Trajan: square modular E, four-bar M, bowed-O. Rigorous grid, wide letter-spacing, no tracking-by-feel. Reference: Parthenon-frieze inscriptions, Athenian stelai of the Marathon dead.

**Body register — Hesiod-cadence chorus-margin.** Body flavor set in a typeface that reads as "academic-painting margin" — the painted legend below a Cabanel or Bouguereau salon-painting. Slightly taller than wide, mild bracketed serif, generous leading. Reference: Musée d'Orsay museum-cards; 19th-c Salon catalogue margins.

**Cadence — dactylic hexameter.** Every flavor line scans long-short-short, long-short-short, six feet. Not metronomic — Hesiod allows substitution — but the ear must find the pulse on the second read. Chorus and gods speak in dactyls; mortals speak in dactyls *broken* (caesura mid-line) when in Lampblack-distress.

**Inscriptional ground.** Doric column-flute + stoichedon-grid frame Legendary and Ultimate card titles. Common and Rare title-frame is the kantharos lip — single-stroke painted band, obsidian-black on chalk-white.

## 7. Shell × Universe interaction rules

Per the 8 Shells (PRIME · AETHER · CYBER · SAINT · MIRROR · HOLLOW · DREAM · MODERN), each gets a binding ruling for Greek Myth.

**PRIME — mandatory, every figure.** Default classical register. Persephone-PRIME = *Hymn to Demeter* maiden in the meadow with the narcissus. Achilles-PRIME = the wrath-figure of *Iliad* book 1. No exceptions.

**AETHER — NATIVE REGISTER. Greek figures *invented* AETHER.** Olympians operate at universal-principle scale by default. Zeus-AETHER = sky-itself. Demeter-AETHER = grain-cycle as cosmic body. Aphrodite-AETHER = desire-as-gravitation. **Reservation: home register for the Olympian dozen at Legendary+ (see §9); for mortals, AETHER is reservation-locked (Council approval required).**

**CYBER — works cleanly.** Achilles-CYBER = kill-team operator with a single hardware vulnerability. Athena-CYBER = wisdom-substrate, city-as-network. Hephaestus-CYBER native (the original engineer). **Test: does the Spine survive substrate-translation?** Yes for Achilles, Odysseus, Athena, Hephaestus, Hermes. No for Persephone (botanical-cyclical), Dionysus (intoxication, not infrastructure), Demeter (grain-cycle, not data-cycle).

**SAINT — NATIVE REGISTER. Greek figures invented SAINT.** The temple-cult-statue *is* the SAINT shell. Athena-of-Athens is patron-saint of the city; Apollo-of-Delphi patron-saint of prophecy. No reservation for Olympians. Mortals only at Legendary, Council-approved: Achilles-SAINT (patron of the short-bright-life), Odysseus-SAINT (homecoming), Penelope-SAINT (the long wait).

**MIRROR — RARE EXCEPTION ONLY (per Cosmology Decision 12).** Gender-architecture is foundational to this Pane; flipping removes Lampblack rather than adding. **Hard rule: max one MIRROR-Greek card per Series, voice-justification + Canon Council approval required.** Persephone-MIRROR is structurally illegible (Descent is gendered in source). Athena-MIRROR is the easiest target (already mannish in cult) — still capped.

**HOLLOW — works for two specific figures, blocked for most.** HOLLOW = what remains after the Spine has been undone. Persephone-HOLLOW (eternalised in the Underworld, no return, seeds binding forever) — one of the most powerful HOLLOW cards in any Universe. Achilles-HOLLOW (the pyre after — only ash on the wind, the armor unclaimed) — works. **Blocked: the Olympians proper (too cosmic to be undone); heroes whose Spine *is* the mortal-remainder (Sisyphus is already HOLLOW in PRIME).**

**DREAM — possible for a narrow band.** Works for Hades-as-dreamspace (Underworld as land-of-dreams, Hypnos / Thanatos register), Morpheus himself, and Orpheus-after-Eurydice (the turn-back-at-threshold reads as the dream-rule applying). Blocked for warriors-in-action (Achilles-DREAM illegible) and Olympians-as-cosmic-principles (Zeus-DREAM dilutes).

**MODERN — possible for specific recodings only.** Hades-as-Manhattan-estate-lawyer works ("lord of the after, keeper of the wills"). Hermes-as-courier / dispatch / financial-arbitrageur works. Pandora-as-cybersecurity-incident works. Penelope-as-the-one-who-waits works. **Blocked: any MODERN that puts the Olympians on Wall Street as superhero-pantheon — Marvel collision, hard-locked.** Persephone-MODERN must avoid Lore Olympus cubicle-romance at all costs; frame as descent-into-grief, not cubicle-coffee.

## 8. Per-Tier art-density rubric

Density is binding. Each Tier specifies registers, figure-count, ground, gold-leaf, voice-mode.

**Common — single figure, classical-pose, painted-marble ground.** Registers: classical statuary photo-real OR red-figure pottery line-on-terracotta OR Waterhouse atmospheric-single-figure. Figures: 1. Ground: painted marble OR pottery-ground OR a single landscape element (olive, column, sea-edge). Gold-leaf: zero, except a kylix-rim or victor's wreath. Voice: mode-1 (signed) or mode-2 (artifact/inscription). *Example:* "The Loom" — Penelope alone, lamp-lit, Waterhouse register.

**Rare — single or paired figure, Symbolist density rising, ornament admitted.** Registers: Burne-Jones + Waterhouse + Bouguereau-secondary, OR Caravaggio chiaroscuro on a single figure. Figures: 1-2. Ground: simple but patterned (column-frieze, pottery-figure-band, textile). Gold-leaf: minimal — goddess's eyes, chiton-hem, one coin. Voice: mode-1 dominant; mode-3 admitted. *Example:* "The Stranger at Ithaca" — Odysseus + Argos, golden-hour, gold on the dog's collar only.

**Legendary — full Bouguereau-Burne-Jones-Symbolist deployment.** Registers: Bouguereau + Burne-Jones + Moreau + Caravaggio + Bernini-sculpture; 2-3 visibly co-present. Figures: 2-4 (central + chorus/antagonist/witness). Ground: Symbolist-jeweled — every surface decorated, every textile patterned, every column friezed. Gold-leaf: heavy on divine attributes, eyes, cult-aureoles, weapons. Voice: full mode-3 licensed. *Example:* "Apollo and Daphne" — Bernini-marble photoreal, Bouguereau backdrop, gold-leaf on laurel-becoming-fingertips.

**Ultimate — full register-stack + Alex-Ross-photoreal-mythic-pose.** Registers: Bouguereau + Burne-Jones + Bernini + Caravaggio + classical-statuary tactile-marble + Alex-Ross-photoreal-mythic-pose. *All* registers present — the card reads as if a Caravaggio shield, a Bernini sculpture, and a Bouguereau salon-painting were composited at the same picture-plane. Figures: 3-6 with a clear central figure. Ground: cathedral-density — every square inch is iconography, ornament, gold, or marble. Gold-leaf: full Byzantine deployment including ground-fields. Voice: hieratic mode-3 dominant. *Example:* "Medusa Beheaded" — Caravaggio-shield photo-real, Athena's reflection in the polish, Pegasus emerging from the neck-blood, gold-leaf ground-field, Bernini-marble snake-bodies.

## 9. Per-Parallel reservation rules

Parallels (ARCANA, AETHER, WITNESS, NEON, plus 1/1) overlay onto Tier and Shell.

**ARCANA — Rare-tier-and-up where the cosmic-fate-mathematics is visible.** Reserved for cards whose Spine is *the visibility of moira itself* — the Pythia delivering the oracle, the Fates at the loom, Hesiod's *Theogony*-begetting-cadence, Cassandra speaking truth no one believes. ARCANA-Persephone = seed-count. ARCANA-Achilles = heel-mathematic. Common-tier ARCANA is forbidden.

**AETHER — Legendary+ where the figure operates at god-scale; native register for Olympians.** Home Parallel for the Olympian dozen at Legendary+. Outside the Olympian dozen, Council sign-off required. Cap: max 4 AETHER per Set (one per Olympian sub-cluster); 1 AETHER per mortal per Series.

**WITNESS — RARE here.** This is a Pane of *participants* — even the chorus participates. Works for Melaina the Pythia's Apprentice (§11), for Telemachus before Mentor speaks, for the herald who carries news. Capped at 2 WITNESS per Set.

**NEON — reserved for CYBER-shell only.** Greek-CYBER is the only place NEON is licensed on this Pane. Olympians-as-superhero-rainbow is the Marvel collision — hard-locked. NEON-Achilles-CYBER works (kill-team operator, neon-on-bronze HUD); NEON-Pandora-CYBER works (the box as encrypted-payload). NEON outside CYBER is forbidden.

**1/1 — one per Set, no exceptions.** Each Set's 1/1 is a Council-sign-off card, the Convergence-grade pull. GM-1: *The Sixth Seed* (Persephone holding the seed she has not yet eaten). GM-2: *The Bow Bent* (Odysseus drawing through twelve axes, Penelope watching, Telemachus at the door). GM-3: *The Eagle's Hour* (Prometheus and the bird, the moment before the descent). ## 11. Lampblacker-Spine candidate

**Decision: Melaina, the Pythia's Apprentice.**

Per Synthesis Part 3 §3, every Series-1 Universe gets a single original load-bearing center — figure not in canon, appearing across all four Sets, carrying Lampblack across the Pane. The role is *the priestess-in-training learning the chamber, the gas, the silence between*. The earlier candidate *The Oracle's Translator* (the woman who carried the Pythia's utterances to the supplicants) was rejected: the Apprentice has a *future* (she will become the Pythia, or fail to, or refuse); the Translator has only a present.

**PROPER NOUN ADOPTED PER V2 REVIEW** — was previously *"The Pythia's Apprentice"* (job-description, not character name); see REVIEW-V2-QUALITY.md §2 + §9 Rewrite 1. Three candidates considered: *Klymene* (V2's recommended placeholder; oracular-root, deep PD; rejected — collides too hard with Klymene-mother-of-Prometheus in canon), *Iola* (Pythian-resonance via Iolaus / Apollo-cult; rejected — too close to Iolaus the warrior), *Melaina* (selected — Greek for *"the dark one"*, literally encodes the Lampblack-residue function in the name; PD; gives a Spine-marker that no other Greek-myth property is using; pairs cleanly with the Apollo-Pythia chamber as the Lampblack of the smoke). The job-title *"the Pythia's Apprentice"* survives in-fiction as the chorus's term for her function, but Melaina is her name.

**Decision rationale (preserved):** the Apprentice has a *future*. She also reads as a closer Lampblack-twin to Hester Quill (BS), the Cartographer (EK), the Glass-Polisher / Court Recorder of Hearts (Wonderland), and Asha Caedmon, the Ledger-Keeper of Whitby (Gothic) — all *figures who maintain the structure the named figures move through*. ## 14. Cultural-sensitivity gates

Greek myth carries baked-in gender violence: Zeus's serial assaults (Leda, Europa, Io, Callisto, Danae); Apollo / Daphne; Hades / Persephone; Poseidon / Medusa. Modern feminist retellings (Haynes, Saint, Barker, Miller) have made the violence visible; audiences now read these stories with that lens. Named gates:

**Gate 1: do not aestheticize assault as romance.** The Lore-Olympus / "Hades is sexy" read is culturally laminated among 18-30 women but contested by classicists as aestheticization of abduction. LoreVault's collector base (financial-gambler-collectors per CEO context) skews male and likely doesn't want either pole. Stay in the *Hymn to Demeter* register: descent as loss, return as conditional, seed as binding. **Tragic, not romantic. Not didactic, not preachy.**

**Gate 2: Apollo / Daphne is transformation-and-grief, not chase-as-comedy.** Bernini is the visual anchor — terror in her face, leaves in her fingers. The flavor line registers her *agency* in asking the river-father for the change.

**Gate 3: Zeus's affairs depicted via consequences, not acts.** Card subjects are the children, the punishments visited on the women, the cult-foundations downstream — not the swans-bulls-showers themselves. *Io as cow*, not Zeus-as-cloud-on-Io. *Leda's daughters* (Helen, Clytemnestra), not the swan-encounter. *Callisto-as-bear*, not the divine moment. The Pane shows the *moira-after*, not the divine-during.

**Gate 4: Medusa uses Ovid's framing.** The curse-by-Athena-after-Poseidon-rape framing is Ovid *Metamorphoses* 4 — deep PD, the modern feminist anchor. The card-text never names "rape"; the frame is unambiguous. Registered draft for the Curse-Moment in GM-4: *"In Athena's temple, Poseidon would not be refused. The temple kept its silence; the goddess did not. The curse fell on the priestess. The curse was the silence made visible."*

**Gate 5: modern Greek-diaspora cultural ownership — minor concern.** Greek myth is treated as world-cultural-patrimony, not Greek-national-IP; modern Greece has not asserted ownership claims. Light caution: don't play the gods as buffoons (the Disney-Hercules problem). **Classical-statuary register is the respectful register.** No advisor retainer required for Series 1.

**Gate 6: gender-violence disclosure on the Wiki.** Short editorial note on the LoreVault wiki Greek Myth page naming that source material includes baked-in gender violence and LoreVault's editorial position is the Bernini-Caravaggio-De-Morgan register — visible-as-tragic, not romantic or comedic. Wiki entry, not card-text. Required pre-Series-1-launch.

## 15. Voice register rules

**The bronze-tongue.** Greek Myth flavor speaks with a tongue cast in bronze — heavy, ringing, slow to change shape. Aphorism is the dominant rhetorical mode. The gods do not chat. The chorus does not chat. Mortals in-frame do not chat — they speak as if they had been speaking for two thousand years and might have to keep speaking for two thousand more. Every line is *cut*, not *flowing*.

**Hesiod-cadence dactylic hexameter.** Long-short-short, long-short-short, six feet. *"Six were the seeds, and the year was a husband and not a husband"* scans. *"Then Persephone ate six of the pomegranate seeds"* is prose and is wrong. **Every flavor line on every Greek Myth card must scan once before approval.** Council gate.

**Chorus-narrator dominant.** First-person plural ("we") over first-person singular ("I"). The Pane has no "I" — only the chorus, the Pythia (second-person to the supplicant), and the figures (third-person). **Melaina (the Pythia's Apprentice — see §11) is the *only* first-person-singular voice on this Pane, and her voice is reserved.**

**Capitalized abstract nouns when gods are doing the noun's work.** Hunger, Memory, Return, Moira, Wrath, Pity, Hospitality (*Xenia*), Hubris. When Demeter is doing Hunger, write *Hunger*; when mortals are hungry, write *hunger*. Apply liberally — Greek Myth is the Pane that reifies abstractions.

**Ordinal time markers.** *The third spring, the seventh libation, the tenth year, the hundredth dawn, the four-hundredth turn of the loom.* Never "later" or "soon" or "after a while." The chorus knows the count.

**Ellipsis-openings allowed and encouraged.** *"…and the seed was already eaten when the seed was offered."* The ellipsis-opening puts the reader inside a sentence that began before they arrived — the register of the Pane (cosmic-debt began before the figure was born; see §1).

**Mode distribution — mode-3 dominant** (this is the Pane's signature; other Universes run mode-1 dominant):
- Mode-1 (signed / character / first-person): ~30%. The Apprentice; mortals in interior moments.
- Mode-2 (in-world artifact / inscription / object-text): ~20%. Stelae, oracle-tablet, kantharos-inscription, herald's report, sailor's prayer.
- Mode-3 (chorus / hieratic / oracular / Hesiodic begetting-cadence): ~50%.

**Banned vocabulary (production-locked):** *destiny* (use *moira* / *the share-out* / *the portion* / *the thread already spun*); *epic*; *legendary* as adjective; *magic / magical / dreamy / spooky / creepy / wonder / whimsy* (Greek Myth is not magical — it is *moira*-bound, which is heavier); *awesome / instinct / vibe / gut / totally / energy / frankly*; modern intensifiers (very, really, super); *cute / sweet / adorable* (see Lore-Olympus inversion).
- *romance, love-story, slow-burn, meet-cute* — banned in any Persephone-Hades context.

**Permitted register-distinct vocabulary:** *moira, kleos, xenia, hubris, ate, nemesis, pothos, eros (cosmic principle only, never romantic feeling), thanatos, hypnos, anangke, peplos, chiton, kantharos, kylix, phiale, agora, polis, oikos, daimon, kore, Eleusis, Mysteries, asphodel, narcissus, omphalos, tripod, libation, oracle, chorus, herald, suitor, shroud, threshold, descent, the seed, the stone, the shield, the loom.*

Where LO is webtoon-flat-color, we are oil-paint and marble. Where it is pink-and-blue romance, we are pomegranate-red and obsidian-black tragedy. Where it is corporate-CEO-Hades, we are Bernini-marble-finger-on-thigh Hades. Where it is slow-burn-romance, we are *moira*-bound cosmic debt.
