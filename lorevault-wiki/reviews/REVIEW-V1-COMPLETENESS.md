## 9. Verdict

**STRENGTHEN-AND-SHIP.**

The bundle is structurally complete. Every mandated section is present in all 5 Bibles. Every Universe has a Manifest with 80 base-PRIME cells plus parallel/shell variants, 1/1 ONE-OFFs locked one-per-Set, Lampblacker-Spine deployments named and threaded, cross-tethers wired to Synthesis Part 3 §1, and cultural-sensitivity gates carried in negative-prompts. The CEO's 5 binding directives are addressed; 4 of 5 V2 Rewrites fully applied (the 5th in substance not label); the 5-Universe lock holds; the ARCANA-overprevalence failure-mode is pre-empted at 3 enforcement layers; the 10 PHASE-05 §15 gates each tie to a bundle artifact.

Three specific gap-closures recommended before V2 quality review proceeds:

1. **Wonderland Bible §15 — add Mode-1/2/3 nomenclature pass.** Substance of HANDOVER §9 #5 is present; the label "Mode-2 philosophical register" is missing. Closing this is a cross-reference edit aligning BIBLE-03 §15 with the BIBLE-01 §15 Mode-1/2/3 schema for cross-Universe register-taxonomy uniformity.

2. **MANIFEST-03 + MANIFEST-04 Part 2 + MANIFEST-05 — add per-row `Validate:` field** (or explicit `assertParallelEligible(cell)` call per row). MANIFEST-01 and MANIFEST-04 P1 have it per-row; the others assert at Set-level table or universal-block reference. Make verifier-ergonomics symmetric so a daemon scanning any Manifest can short-circuit on a single-row predicate.

3. **Iceberg-audit cell-flag table** — for each Series, explicitly tabulate which 12 cells are Echo-tier and which 24 are Deep-tier (HANDOVER §9 #4). Framework lives in PHASE-05 §13 + Synthesis Part 3 §1; the flag is structurally derivable but not enumerated. Add a single cross-Manifest table (Synthesis Part 4 candidate) so the Council Iceberg-Auditor (PHASE-05 §15 gate 5) has a one-page pre-launch artifact.

None of the three are ship-blocking. Each is closeable in a single editorial pass per artifact. Once closed, V2 (quality review) proceeds against a structurally complete bundle.

Completeness reviewed. — V1, 2026-04-26.
