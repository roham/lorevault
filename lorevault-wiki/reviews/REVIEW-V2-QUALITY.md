## 1. Does the Universe Identity Sing?

**Test:** Open each Bible at §1, read the first 200 words cold, and ask — *would I screenshot a sentence from this and send it to a friend with no caption?*

### Baker Street — STRONG.
Strongest line: *"Holmes is the Lattice's epistemologist: the figure who arrives at every other Pane to ask, 'How do you know?'"* That single sentence carries the whole Universe — tells me what the Pane *does cosmologically* in the voice of someone who has thought about it. The "Pane of Argument" framing earns its keep — *deduction-itself-as-cosmological-role* is precisely the MTG-color-pie-equivalent move Innistrad makes. Bones land.
Weakest: the parenthetical *"(Reichenbach, the failed prophecy at Delphi, the Cottingley fairies that don't yield)"* is *inside* the second paragraph — a lateral move to other Panes before this Pane has fully landed. **Fix:** push to §12 cross-tether map.
**MTG comparison:** Innistrad-tier.

### Enchanted Kingdom — STRONGEST OF THE FIVE.
Strongest line: *"Snow White's apple is also Pandora's jar, also Eden's apple, also the apple of Eris, also Wonderland's Drink-Me. The Pane of Inheritance is where the Lattice reveals that every fruit-of-binding is the same fruit refracted."* That's the bundle's single best Universe-identity sentence — does the Lampblack work *inside* the identity statement, reads at once Pratchett-ironic and MTG-totalist.
Weakest: *"the wolf is right to be hungry and the stepmother is right to be jealous, and the cards are not asking you to forgive either of them."* Comma-comma flattens what should be two impacts. **Fix:** break to periods.
**MTG comparison:** Innistrad-tier; the additional virtue of being upstream of all four other S1 Panes is the bundle's biggest credibility deposit.

### Wonderland — STRONG ON AMBITION, COLD ON LANDING.
Strongest line: *"The Hatter's six o'clock tea is a logical solution to a punishment by Time-as-a-person."* The Universe in a fortune-cookie.
Weakest: opener *"Wonderland is the Lattice's fault zone"* — the metaphor *"fault zone"* is the most generic move in the document. **Fix:** *"Wonderland is the Pane where the categories the other Universes lean on stop loadbearing."*
**MTG comparison:** below Kamigawa-tier on the identity passage; Liddell-logic and pun-prohibition discipline are excellent, but §1 is the Bible's softest opener.

### Gothic Horror — STRONGEST DOCTRINAL ANCHOR.
Strongest line: *"The body as betrayer. The mirror that fails. The ledger that survives the wreck."* That's a tagline. The bullet-list of seven figures whose bodies have stopped being theirs (Dracula doesn't reflect; Mina has the wafer-burn; the Creature was never one body) is the bundle's single highest-density block.
Weakest: §1 carries IP-ops business inside Universe-identity prose (*"Universal Pictures sits as the most-litigious studio…"*). **Fix:** §1 doctrinal Pane-statement only; cliff-clearance lives in §13 where it already does.
**MTG comparison:** Innistrad-tier and the closest deliverable in the bundle to an actual Magic plane.

### Greek Myth — GOOD, REGISTER-RISK.
Strongest line: *"Every Greek Myth Moment must read as the inside of a ledger that began before the figure was born and continues after."*
Weakest: the *un-Lore-Olympus* paragraph is too long for §1 — the most defensive paragraph in the bundle in the most front-of-house position. The reader does not yet know what Lore Olympus is and the Pane shouldn't be defining itself negatively against it on page one. **Fix:** keep one inversion sentence in §1; move the rest to §15.
**MTG comparison:** Theros-tier. Cosmic-debt-as-ledger is *better* than Theros's fate-as-stars because debt is more specific than fate.

**§1 Verdict:** four of five sing; Wonderland and Greek are surgical-fix problems, not structural.

## 2. The Lampblacker-Spine Test

**The Footnote (BS).** Concept correct: the clerk who copies Watson's casebook into the *Strand* layout IS the Lattice's archive-function for Baker Street. Spine line *"Every case has a margin. I am what is written there."* is screenshot-able. **But the name is the problem — "The Footnote" reads as job description, not character.** It will not get quote-tweeted. The only of the five Spines without a proper noun. **Recommended:** *Edith Wright* (Wright the corrector; period-correct middle-class clerical name; Spencerian-cipher initials EW). **Verdict:** load-bearing concept; ornamental name. Rename before Series-2.

**The Charcoal-Burner's Daughter (EK).** Bible upgraded the Synthesis's "Cartographer" to this and was right to. The literal-charcoal-as-Pane's-Lampblack-ink connection is the bundle's most rigorous Lampblack mechanic. Spine line *"The wolf walked past me. I had already drawn his shadow."* is one of the two best Spine lines in the bundle. **Name problem:** six syllables, four words. Quote-tweet and binder-cover marketing will not survive this length. **Fix:** keep the role, but *give her a proper name* by EK-3 reveal (*"They called her Marit"* / *"Ada"* / *"Jovanka"*). One handle that fits in 32 chars. **Verdict:** strongest concept; supplement-name needed.

**The Glass-Polisher / Court Recorder of Hearts (WL).** *Conceptually best of the five.* Two-byline-system (Glass-Polisher in private; Court Recorder of Hearts in public) is the bundle's most original Spine. Voice exemplars (*"I keep the surface clear. What looks back is not my doing"*) ship as Tumblr-screenshot fodder *today*. **But:** dual-naming is a fan-cohesion liability — Tumblr will pick one and the other will atrophy. **Fix:** Council should commit to Court Recorder of Hearts as public byline; keep Glass-Polisher as in-fiction proper noun; stop offering both as equal alternatives.

**Asha Caedmon (GH).** *The standard against which the others should be measured.* Full proper name; surname inherits the 7th-c. Whitby-Saxon-poet (England's first named poet — the *archive-of-the-spoken* exactly the Pane's load). First name has religious-literary weight (Avestan *truth/righteous-order*; tethers to the Daroga without Stoker's Romani-coded other). Spine line *"I came up from the wreck of the Demeter. I have been writing it down ever since"* is the bundle's single best Spine line. The 2027 Liberation Drop turns her into the *vehicle of the entire press-wave*. **Verdict:** no fix.

**The Pythia's Apprentice (Greek).** Same problem as The Footnote. Role is correct and load-bearing; she is the only first-person-singular voice on the Pane, by design — strong mechanical asymmetry. Cross-Set deployment workable. **But: no proper noun.** Tumblr will not adopt "the Pythia's Apprentice" as a tag. **Fix:** name her — *Klymene* (oracular root, deep PD) or *Iola* or *Ourania*. One proper noun is what closes the Asha-Caedmon-tier gap.

**§2 Verdict:** Two Spines (Asha, Charcoal-Burner's Daughter) at *thrilled*; one (Glass-Polisher / Recorder) at *thrilled* if dual-naming gets disciplined; two (Footnote, Apprentice) are *load-bearing concepts dressed as working-titles*. The bundle MUST give them proper nouns before Series-2 — the cross-Pane Lampblacker-Index card needs five names that read as a *roster*, not an *index entry list*.

**The Pratchett-Discoverer (23).** Card: **EK-1 Ultimate 1/1 — *The Footnoter Reveals Themselves*** (Snow-Black Topsy-mode 1/1; the Footnoter's hand entering the margin from below; the only EK card that breaks the fourth wall). ## 6. The Cross-Universe Discipline Test

Per Dossier #21: *"Every property that treated crossovers as the headline collapsed; every property that treated them as the secret beneath the headline endured."* Series 1 declared budget: 0 Surface, 1-2 Echoes, 0 Convergence (Synthesis §4.3 / Phase-05 §14.1).

Audit: BS ships 1 Echo (Cottingley) + 1 META-shadow (Hamlet watermark; correct META-Universe register). EK ships 0 Surface, 1 Echo (apple ↔ pomegranate). Wonderland ships its Universe 1-of-1 with Lampblack-silver showing four other Panes (permitted by doctrine — Looking-Glass IS the canonical-internal explanation for cross-Pane traversal) plus Cheshire-grin-in-negative-space on one card per other Universe. GH Part 2 explicitly ships zero cross-Pane cards (cleanest discipline in bundle). Greek ships zero cross-Pane Surfaces or Echoes.

**Concern flag — TAXONOMY COLLISION.** Wonderland Bible §12 declares Cheshire-grin-cross-Pane on one card per other Universe = *4 cross-Pane Echoes*. Phase-05 §14.1 caps Series 1 cross-Pane Echoes at **1-2 across the entire Series**. This is a genuine doctrinal collision.

**Recommended resolution:** classify Cheshire-grin-in-negative-space as **Pattern 9 / Deep / Lampblack Trade** (no figure crosses; only residue) per Dossier #21 §2. Pattern 9 is *"the lowest-stakes, highest-density crossover Moments — should make up the bulk of crossover production."* This protects Phase-05's Echo cap and preserves Bible §12's Cheshire-as-Lampblack-incarnate doctrine. Per-Series Deep budget is 0-4 → 4 Cheshire residues fits perfectly.

No cell I audited is a cross-Universe Surface dressed as an Echo. **§6 Verdict:** discipline holds; one taxonomy reconciliation (15 minutes of Council seat 4 work).

## 7. The Voice-Register Mosaic Test

Spot-check on Wonderland. Seven flavor lines in sequence:

1. *"The cat was not there. The grin had not been informed."* (WL-1 U)
2. *"The pool was her own. The Mouse, the Lory, the Eaglet, and the Dodo were also hers, in the manner that her tears were hers."* (WL-1 L)
3. *"The Rabbit checked his watch. The watch was not the issue. The Rabbit was late for the issue, and the issue had not waited."* (WL-1 C)
4. *"The clock was at six. The Hatter was at six. The Time who had punished them had moved on."* (WL-2 U)
5. *"The Hatter poured tea. The cup did not fill. The cup had agreed not to."* (WL-2 L)
6. *"The verdict came first. The trial took longer, because the verdict had to be defended."* (WL-3 L)
7. *"The pawn went forward one square. Then another. Then she was a queen, and the squares were a kingdom, and the kingdom was a game she had not been told she was playing."* (WL-4 U)

**Mosaic test result: PASSING — barely.** What lands: every line is logic-puzzle-under-nonsense-surface, italicized-categorical-words or third-person Recorder-deadpan, no pun, no exclamation. Each unmistakably Wonderland.

**Wallpaper risk:** the *three-beat statement-restatement-snap* structure shows up in five of seven sampled lines. Read in sequence the pattern becomes audible. Tumblr will pick up on it within month 4 and start memeing the structure.

**Fix:** Council seat 2a/2b should audit WL-2 + WL-3 flavor sets and replace ≥2 lines per Set with the *Q:/A:* courtroom-transcript variant the Bible §15 also licenses. *"Q: Where is the Cheshire Cat? A: Elsewhere. Q: Then what is here?"* breaks the three-beat-snap rhythm.

**§7 Verdict.** Passes line-quality dimension; just-barely passes structure-variety. Audit + 2 line-replacement fixes pre-mint.
