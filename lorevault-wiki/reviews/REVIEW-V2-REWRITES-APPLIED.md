## Rewrite 1 — Rename the two ornamental Spines

V2 finding (REVIEW-V2-QUALITY.md §2): *The Footnote* (Baker Street) and *The Pythia's Apprentice* (Greek Myth) are job-descriptions, not character names. Tumblr will not adopt them as quote-tweet handles; the cross-Pane Lampblacker-Index card (Series 2-3 marquee) needs the five Spines to read as a *roster*, not an *index entry list*. Both concepts are load-bearing; only the names are ornamental.

### Baker Street — *The Footnote* → **Hester Quill**

Three candidates evaluated. **Edith Wright** (V2's placeholder — credible but plain). **Margaret Lambeth** (geographic tether to Strand; weaker function-as-name encoding). **Hester Quill** *(SELECTED)* — surname encodes the writing-implement function (*the quill-as-tool* literally lives in her name); H-initial rhymes with Hudson (one room over from 221B); period-appropriate 1880s-1900s English middle-class clerical (Hester for the H, period-Quills as clerk-surnames); female-leaning per V2 confirmation; 221B-adoptable as a quote-tweet handle.

The job-title *"the Footnote"* survives as the Council's nickname for her work and as the BS-4 Common Card 9 *card title* (*"The Footnote at the Margin"*). Her **public-facing identity remains the cipher *HQ*** rendered as a calligraphic Spencerian flourish — never spelled phonetically on Series-1 cards. Her name is given in flavor only.

### Greek Myth — *The Pythia's Apprentice* → **Melaina**

Three candidates evaluated. **Klymene** (V2's placeholder; oracular-root, deep PD — rejected: collides with Klymene-mother-of-Prometheus in canon, and the Apprentice carries the Prometheus-question to the Pythia in GM-3). **Iola** (Pythian-resonance via Iolaus/Apollo-cult — rejected: too close to Iolaus the warrior; not function-encoding). **Melaina** *(SELECTED)* — Greek for *"the dark one"*. **Literally encodes the Lampblack-residue function in the name.** PD; Greek-authentic; no other property in the modern Greek-myth collector market is using it (Lore Olympus, Hadestown, Hades II, Riordan all clear). Pairs cleanly with Apollo's chamber: Melaina is the Lampblack of the smoke; the Pythia is the chamber the smoke fills.

The job-title *"the Pythia's Apprentice"* survives as the chorus's term for her function; Melaina is her name on the cards and in flavor.

## Rewrite 2 — GH-3 #20 *The Persian's Footnote* image-anchor

V2 finding (§4 + §9 Rewrite 2): the only 1/1 in the bundle that does not clear *thrilled*. Concept correct (Persian deposition + Asha annotation + Erik mask) but the picture-plane is *three text-elements stacked* — typography-on-typography-on-typography. The Sherlockian completist will screenshot for the *words*; the gambler-collector will not screenshot for the *painting* — because as a painting it is flat. No Bouguereau-tier figural moment.

### Edit

`MANIFEST-04-GOTHIC-HORROR-PART2-SETS-3-4.md` — GH-3 row 20 cell expanded to include a single named figural anchor. Both the row's **figure-and-iconography column** and the **Registered flavor (binding)** block were updated.

### Before — figure-and-iconography column (truncated)

> Erik's death-mask in profile (foreground); Daroga's witness-paragraph rendered in opera-libretto-margin typography (mid-ground); Asha Caedmon's annotation visible at page-bottom in Asylum-Ledger Clinical hand: *"Erik. Real. The Persian writes from Paris. The mask lies in my drawer."*

### After — figure-and-iconography column (truncated)

> **FIGURAL ANCHOR (per V2 Review Rewrite 2 — added 2026-04-26):** the Persian's (Daroga's) hand resting on the brass-fitted prompter's-box rail of the Palais Garnier opera house; a single candle's flame reflected in the polished brass fitting; the score-page open at the unmarked aria *(the canonical Leroux figural moment — the Persian deposing as eyewitness from inside the opera-house architecture)*. Erik's death-mask in profile (foreground, set on the prompter's-box edge beside the candle); Daroga's witness-paragraph rendered in opera-libretto-margin typography (mid-ground, the score-page text); Asha Caedmon's annotation visible at page-bottom in Asylum-Ledger Clinical hand…

### Before — registered flavor

> *"Erik was a man. The Daroga writes from Paris and the page does not contradict him. — A.C., Whitby Abbey, 22 March 1928."*

### After — registered flavor

> *"Erik was a man. The Daroga's hand rested on the prompter's-box rail; the candle in the brass fitting did not flicker. The page does not contradict him. — A.C., Whitby Abbey, 22 March 1928."*

### Why this works

The *prompter's-box* is canon-Leroux: in *Le Fantôme de l'Opéra*, the Persian is the eyewitness inside the Garnier whose deposition Leroux's narrator authenticates. Putting his *hand* on the *rail* — with the *candle* reflected in the *brass fitting* and the *score-page* open at the *unmarked aria* — gives the picture-plane four named figural anchors: a hand, a flame, a brass surface, an open page. That is enough Bouguereau-tier figural density for the gambler-collector's screenshot. The textual stack (Persian's deposition + Asha's annotation + the death-mask in profile) survives intact; the figural anchor is *added*, not *replacing*. Lifts the cell from *interesting* to *thrilled*.

## Rewrite 5 — Wonderland WL-2 + WL-3 flavor: break three-beat-snap repetition

V2 finding (§7 + §9 Rewrite 5): five of seven sampled Wonderland flavor lines hit the same *statement-restatement-snap* structure. Mosaic Test passes today; will fail in month 4 when Tumblr starts memeing the cadence. V2's prescription: replace ≥2 lines per Set with the *Q:/A: courtroom-transcript* variant Bible §15 already licenses, OR with structurally distinct alternatives (question→answer-that-undoes / definition-offered-and-immediately-undone / categorical-italic-marker / second-person-Queen-threat).

### Audit method

Sampled WL-2 + WL-3 pullquotes and lore-notes. Four flavor-text fields share identical three-beat statement-restatement-snap rhythm: WL-2-U1 pullquote, WL-2-L1 pullquote, WL-3-U1 lore-note (Court Recorder gloss), WL-3-L1 pullquote.

The two 1/1 marquee pullquotes (WL-2-U1 + WL-3-U1) are working *for* the Pane and per V2 §1 audit are clean exemplars of the bronze-tongue cadence. Editing those risks degrading the marquee. The three edits below target the two Legendary pullquotes (WL-2-L1, WL-3-L1) and the WL-3-U1 *lore-note* (the Court Recorder's secondary gloss, not the marquee Alice line).

### Edit 1 — WL-2-L1 *The Mad Tea-Party* pullquote → question→answer-that-undoes

#### Before

> *The Hatter poured tea. The cup did not fill. The cup had agreed not to.*

#### After

> *Q: Is this tea? A: It was tea before you asked.*

Q:/A: courtroom-transcript variant Bible §15 licenses. Liddell-logic preserved (the asking-changes-the-answer is exactly the categorical-failure WL-2 runs on). Pun-prohibition observed.

### Edit 2 — WL-3-L1 *The Trial of the Knave of Hearts* pullquote → definition-offered-and-immediately-undone

#### Before

> *The verdict came first. The trial took longer, because the verdict had to be defended.*

#### After

> *A trial — by definition — is the* event *in which a verdict is* discovered. *Strike that.*

Definition-offered-and-immediately-undone structure. Italicized-categorical-words preserved per Bible §15. The legal *"Strike that"* is the Court Recorder's voice cancelling the categorical anchor she just laid down — exactly Wonderland's spine (a category fails, a small unflappable figure notices, the failure becomes the point).

### Edit 3 — WL-3-U1 *You're Nothing But a Pack of Cards* lore-note → second-person Queen-threat / Q:/A:

#### Before

> *The court has heard the verdict. The court has not yet heard the trial. The court will hear it, in due time, if there is time. — The Court Recorder of Hearts*

#### After

> *Off with your head, said the Queen. Whose? said the deck. — The Court Recorder of Hearts*

Second-person Queen-threat (canonical *"Off with [your] head"* in voice; not quoting Carroll's exact phrasing) crossed with deck's question-talkback Q:/A:. The deck answering back IS the categorical-failure the Pane runs on. Two structural moves in one line (Queen-threat + Q:/A:) where the previous version had only one (statement-restatement-snap).

### Resulting distribution

After the three edits, WL-2 + WL-3 flavor carries: 2× three-beat-snap (preserved on the 1/1 marquees), 1× Q:/A:, 1× definition-undone, 1× Queen-threat-with-deck-talkback, 1× ellipsis-elliptical (WL-3-R5, already varied), 1× categorical-italic-marker (WL-2-R4, already varied). Structural variety doubled; *month 4* meme-risk defused.
