## Notes on the Five — what together they demonstrate

Together these five cards exercise the working surfaces of LoreVault as a single system. **Universe coverage is one-each across all five Panes** — Baker Street (Card 1), Enchanted Kingdom (Card 2), Wonderland (Card 3), Gothic Horror (Card 4), Greek Myth (Card 5). **Tier distribution is exactly the briefed 1C / 2R / 1L / 1U** — Card 1 a Common at 15,000 mint, Cards 2 and 3 the two Rares at 50 (NEON-parallel) and 500 (ARCANA-parallel) respectively, Card 4 a Legendary at WITNESS-parallel mint of 10, Card 5 the Ultimate at AETHER-parallel mint of 3.

**Shell range is five distinct shells across five cards** — PRIME, MIRROR, DREAM, HOLLOW, SAINT — exceeding the ≥3-shell requirement and making the point that the eight-shell taxonomy is alive, not theoretical. Notably, four of the five are non-PRIME, which models the Series 2 "shell expansion" thesis precisely. The PRIME on Card 1 is the launch-canon anchor that justifies all four expansions: a collector who owns Card 1 *and* Card 2's Snow Black sees the same Apple-Spine running across PRIME and MIRROR shells.

**Parallel range is four non-base parallels plus base** — base (Card 1), NEON (Card 2), ARCANA (Card 3), WITNESS (Card 4), AETHER (Card 5) — exceeding the ≥2 non-base requirement and explicitly excluding the redundant pairings (no AETHER-shell × AETHER-parallel, no CYBER × NEON) per the §1 collapsing-redundancy rule of MULTIVERSE-SHELLS. Mint counts are calculated correctly per the economy spec's §4 table: Rare-NEON = 50, Rare-ARCANA = 500, Legendary-WITNESS = 10, Ultimate-AETHER = 3, Common-base = 15,000.

**Voice range covers all five universe-registers** — Watson-chronicling forensic-confessional (Card 1), Enchanted Kingdom bone-rhyme couplet (Card 2), Wonderland Liddell-logic wisecrack (Card 3), Gothic Horror epistolary logbook (Card 4), Greek Myth bronze-tongue chorus echo (Card 5). **Archetype range covers five of the twelve** — A1 Deduction, A4 Curse, A9 Wisecrack-That-Lands, A6 Logbook, A12 Echo — sampling the spread without exhausting it.

**Tether range is two explicit cross-set tethers** — Card 2 (EK-6 Snow Black ↔ EK-4 The Apple and the Mirror, with the same half-bitten apple as visible Lampblack), and Card 5 (GM-10 Saint of Return ↔ GM-1 The Descent, with the third-spring fragment quoted in italics as A12 Echo, tied also to EK-12's threshold-stone in the lore note). Card 4's flavor includes the Lampblacker as document attribution — an Echo-tether toward GH-7 The Lampblacker, the Decision-11-mandated original anchor — without making the Lampblacker the figure depicted.

**The Ultimate (Card 5) demonstrates the SAINT'S-GLIMPSE shell-crossing mechanism** per MULTIVERSE-SHELLS §4.3 — two flanking shell-silhouettes (Persephone-PRIME and Persephone-HOLLOW) rendered as iconographic attributes only, in Lampblack itself, neither as live figure. Maximum of two observed; both rendered as symbols, not live agents; the audience-facing phrase "the Lampblack thickens" appears in the flavor text exactly per §4 of the shells doctrine.

**What this five-card sample does NOT cover.** Three shells absent — CYBER, MODERN, AETHER (as shell, not as parallel). Seven archetypes absent — A2 Prophecy, A3 Lover's Letter, A5 Riddle, A7 Hesiod Catalog, A8 Pamphlet, A10 Last Word, A11 Anonymous Witness. The 1/1 ONE-OFF parallel is not exercised. The Vertigo-Moment and Dream Hand-Off shell-crossing mechanisms (the other two of three sanctioned per §4) are not demonstrated. No card uses the multi-narrator-rotation discipline within a single Set (Decision 6) — these are five cards from five different Sets, so cross-narrator tension is between Sets, not within. No Series-3 lattice-touchpoint *landscape*-as-Ultimate is exercised (Card 5 is a SAINT-Ultimate, not a touchpoint-Ultimate). All omissions are deliberate and proportional: a five-card sample cannot cover the full eight-shell × four-tier × five-parallel × twelve-archetype catalog without becoming a sampler that exercises everything shallowly. These five exercise enough of the system to *prove* it ships, and leave enough headroom to *prove* it has more story to tell.
