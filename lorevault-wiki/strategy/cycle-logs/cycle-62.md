> Lampblack acts. It is residue in origin but force in effect — a pressure one Pane exerts on its nearest counterparts through accumulated contact. Sherlock-PRIME, reading a case file in 1895, pauses at a word he does not know and feels, for less than a second, that he has reached for something in a darkened room — a visor, perhaps, equipment with no 1895 analogue — and found it not there. He writes *proceed* and continues. He will not remember it. What pressed through was Lampblack: not his, but Sherlock-CYBER's, bleeding faintly across the Pane-boundary and touching the figure closest to the gestural signature of that reach. Lampblack does not speak; it does not redirect. It exerts — a gravity, a habitual weight, a flicker in the peripheral register of a mind entirely inside its own Pane. This is the distinction that matters for downstream design: Lampblack is not only the *evidence* that two shells share a figure; it is the *mechanism* by which they remain aware of each other at the threshold of perception. A card that carries Lampblack correctly will make the collector feel they have seen this figure before. That feeling is not a metaphor. It is the physics working.

## WHY

§9 Rewrite 2 has been deferred since cycle 45 (the cycle in which the Council-lock brief was written). The HANDOVER note reads: "Lampblack as force, not residue — NOT YET APPLIED. Need 1 paragraph in cosmology §1 showing Lampblack ACTING on a figure (e.g., 'Sherlock-PRIME dreams of a visor he cannot remember reaching for')."

The prior doctrine described Lampblack exclusively as residue and recognition-marker — the *evidence* that two shells share a figure (Law Two: "a recurring symbol, prop, line, or scar that survives translation"). This was correct as far as it went, but incomplete. If Lampblack is only evidence, it is inert: something to be read by collectors, not something that happens to figures. The rewrite shifts the register.

The visor paragraph does the required work in one image: Sherlock-PRIME reaches for equipment that belongs to Sherlock-CYBER, finds nothing, writes *proceed*, and moves on. He does not understand what happened. The collector does. The force crossed the Pane-boundary; it did not explain itself; it did not redirect the figure; it left a faint trace in the peripheral register of a mind inside its own Pane. That is the physics — and the paragraph names it explicitly at the close: "That feeling is not a metaphor. It is the physics working."

The structural placement (after Law Two, before Law Three) is deliberate. Law Two establishes what Lampblack is; the new paragraph establishes what it does; Law Three closes the section with the No-Merge prohibition. The three move from definition to mechanism to constraint.
