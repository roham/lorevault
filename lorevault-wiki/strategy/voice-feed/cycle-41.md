By the time he has written the date, the specific content has softened. He writes *Mina* and crosses it out — not wrong, but insufficient. A name without its content is a gesture, not a record. He writes: *13 June — present on waking. Lost in the writing.*
**Card unlock:** *GH-1 Common — "Present on Waking"*

**Card unlock:** *GH-1 Common — "Arrived: Unclear"*

- **June 16 (temporal orientation failed):** Jonathan cannot locate himself in the day without evidence. The threshold sentence closes the gap.
