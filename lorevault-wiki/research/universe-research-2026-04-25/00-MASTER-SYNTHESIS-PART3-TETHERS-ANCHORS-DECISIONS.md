# 3. The Original-Anchor Roster — Lampblacker-Spine Candidates

Public-domain figures alone don't sustain emotional gravity (too pre-loaded, too distributed across rival media). Each Universe needs an original load-bearing center. **Decision: commission all five simultaneously for Series 2 (no staggering — staggered Spines create uneven Universe gravity, per Dossier 21 §10 #4).**

| Universe | Anchor (Lampblacker Spine) | Role | Spine |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Baker Street** | **The Footnote** | Unnamed clerk whose name appears in the margins of every Holmes case. Anchors the *epistolary-witness* role — the silent index of Watson's narratives. | "Every case has a margin. I am what is written there." |
| **Enchanted Kingdom** | **The Cartographer** | The woman who draws the Forest's maps. Maps don't survive in canon fairy tales; she is why. | "What the wolf walks past, I have already drawn." |

| **Gothic Horror** | **The Ledger-Keeper of Whitby** | Original female survivor pulled from the *Demeter* wreck as a child, raised in Whitby Abbey ruin; archives the supernatural across the Stoker / Shelley / Stevenson / Leroux / Le Fanu canon.
